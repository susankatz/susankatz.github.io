The Wine Hunter's Map

An interactive tool for finding perfect wines

...and maybe your next vacation destination!

Every year, Wine Enthusiast Magazine complies and publishes reviews on over 24,000 wines across the globe. In each wine in review, a taste specialist provides a descriptive flavor profiling and documents the product's price, grape variety, and place of orgin. These data points are then ultimately used to determine the wine's "scoring", which is based on a 1 to 100 scale. This experience will focus on the 2017 wine reviews.

The user of the map below will be able to utilize the wine type (aka chardonnay, pinot noir, etc.) and price variables to identify which countries/states/areas produce the highest scoring products. (E.g. California produces the best red blends at the $10-$30 price point)

Once the user has identified the country/area/region in the map above, they can use it in this chart to find the related wine titles and their exact prices/scores.
